Why Tikal is the place to be:

Tikal is an ancient Mayan citadel in the rainforests of northern Guatemala. At 70 meters, Tikal contains the tallest pre-Columbian structure in the Americas. This is a great place for families to adventure through great pyramids, sculptures, temples, and palaces. So this being said, if you enjoy historical monuments and great adventures, Tikal is the place for you!
