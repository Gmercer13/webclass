The Beach: The Most Relaxing Place On Earth!

The beach, in my opinion, is one of the happiest places on Earth. it is a place where people can truly relax and forget about all their responsibilities and any troubles in life. Everyone is always so happy spending time with their families and friends, eating french fries, and just enjoying the sun beaming down.

During the Summer, my family and I spend our time at our beach house in Lewes, Delaware. We enjoy riding our bikes to the beach and listening to music that makes us feel happy. I love spending time in the ocean and soaking up the warm sun after getting out of the cold, yet refreshing water.

The beach really is my happy place and I hope you enjoy it as much as I do.
