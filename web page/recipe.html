Acai Bowls!

"Acai Bowls are the perfect healthy snack to eat on the beach, or even just at your house!"

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 of a banana
  2. 1 100-g of frozen unsweetened acai berry pulp
  3. 1/2 cup of frozen strawberries
  4. 1/2 cup of frozen blueberries
  5. 1/2 cup of unsweetened vanilla almond milk

Toppings:

  • 1/4 cup of blueberries
  • 1/2 kiwi (peel, slice, or chop)
  • 1/4 cup of sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup of granola
  • drizzle of raw honey

    • Directions:

    1. blend all ingredients in a smoothie blender
    2. once it has turned into a smoothie, empty it out into a bowl
    3. simply add the toppings you would like and enjoy:))
